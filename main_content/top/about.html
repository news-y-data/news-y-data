As the name news-Y-data indicates the website provides data that is news-worthy. We primarily publish data that has social, economic or political significance to people all over the world. Many official sources such as governments, university students, research bodies publish data that does not reach an average citizen. In the world driven by data, several institutions including governments, biased media houses try to misrepresent the data and manipulate people.

With the website, we try to bridge that gap by explaining data in simple and vivid language. Meaningful insights will be drawn and presented using graphs and other visualizations .
